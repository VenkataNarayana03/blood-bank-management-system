Eligibility Rules

  • Age must be between 18 and 65 years.
  • Minimum weight is 50 kg.
  • Hemoglobin level must be 12.5 g/dL or above.
  • You must not have chronic diseases or a recent surgery.
  • You must not have taken alcohol or antibiotics in the last 24 hours.

This eligibility check is an initial screening. Medical staff make the final decision before donation.
